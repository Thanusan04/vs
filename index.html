Sri Lanka: An Island of Rich Heritage and Natural Beauty

Sri Lanka, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is an island nation known for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and diverse wildlife. Located just south of India, Sri Lanka boasts a unique blend of ancient traditions and modernity. With its lush tea plantations, pristine beaches, and breathtaking mountains, the island offers a variety of experiences for travelers and locals alike. Sri Lanka is also home to a number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the ancient city of Sigiriya, the sacred city of Kandy, and the historic Galle Fort.
